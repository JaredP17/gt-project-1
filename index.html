Remote Island
How to travel...
Select a getaway using the buttons on the home page. When you select a location, you will be taken to your destination page. You will be presented with a live stream video of a the location of your choice. You will see current weather of your location, and be served a virtual cocktail. Click on the recipe button to learn how to make it yourself. If you are finished with your current vacation and want to travel somewhere new, use the navigation menu to select a new location.
